Srideep Sarkar

Srideep has two and a half decades of diverse and rich corporate experience across industries and a range of Fortune 500 companies including start-ups. This experience has provided him with strong commercial reality and deep understanding of leadership. Srideep has successfully held various HR leadership roles in regional and global contexts. He has also had the opportunity to lead the function as HR head and lead the global agenda for Leadership Development.

Srideep has also received multiple awards for the business impact of development interventions. In his early career, he held business roles in sales and banking environments. This varied experience often helps him to quickly understand clients' unique worlds.
