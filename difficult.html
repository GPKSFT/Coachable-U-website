Why Change is Difficult?

Process of Change is Non-Linear

There are five gaps which stop us from changing. Click to know about the gaps

It is hard to change if you don’t know what to change.

It is difficult to change if you don’t experiment with what you want to change.

It is impossible to change if you don’t reflect on what you learned from experimentation.

It is hard to change unless you reach out to people for support based on insights from reflection.

It is difficult to change unless the momentum gained from collaboration fuels sustainable action.
